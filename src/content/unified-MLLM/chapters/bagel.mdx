### 架构

![BAGEL_architecture](BAGEL_architecture.png)

LLM: Qwen2.5-LLM

Und encoder: Siglip2-so400M (384, NaVit)

projection layer: 2-layer MLP

Gen encoder: VAE of FLUX (downsample ratio=8, 2\*2 patch embedding)

time step:embedding

- Noised VAE tokens: Rectified Flow, MSE loss
- Clean VAE tokens: original latents
- ViT tokens: SigLIP2 encoder

attentions

![BAGEL_self_attention](BAGEL_self_attention.png)

Ablation study: MoT is better than MoE
