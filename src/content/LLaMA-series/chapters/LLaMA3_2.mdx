### Training

1. We add image adapters and encoders, then pretrain on large-scale noisy (image, text) pair data
2. We train on medium-scale high quality in-domain and knowledge-enhanced (image, text) pair data.

### Remark

1. LLM are frozen during training
2. Language support: English, German, French, Italian, Portuguese, Hindi, Spanish, and Thai.
3. Accepts multiple images, but works best on single image input
4. The adapter consists of a series of cross-attention layers that feed image encoder representations into the language model.
5. In post-training, we use a similar recipe as the text models by doing several rounds of alignment on supervised fine-tuning, rejection sampling, and direct preference optimization.

### Remark

- Language support: English, German, French, Italian, Portuguese, Hindi, Spanish, and Thai.
- For the 1B and 3B models, we took the approach of using structured pruning in a single shot manner from the Llama 3.1 8B.
- For the 1B and 3B in Llama 3.2, we incorporated logits from the Llama 3.1 8B and 70B models into the pre-training stage of the model development, where outputs (logits) from these larger models were used as token-level targets
- Each round involves supervised fine-tuning (SFT), rejection sampling (RS), and direct preference optimization (DPO).
