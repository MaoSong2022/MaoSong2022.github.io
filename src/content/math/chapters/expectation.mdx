## Expectation

conditional expectation 的定义如下

$$
\mathbb{E}[X\mid A=a] = \sum_{x}xp(x\mid a)
$$

全概率公式定义如下

$$
\boxed{\mathbb{E}[X] = \sum_{a}\mathbb{E}[X\mid A=a]p(a)} \tag{law of total expectation}
$$

证明如下

$$
\begin{aligned}
\sum_{a}\mathbb{E}[X\mid A=a]p(a) &= \sum_a\left[\sum_xp(x\mid a)x\right]p(a)\\
&= \sum_x\sum_a p(x\mid a)p(a)p(x)\\
&= \sum_x\left[\sum_a p(x\mid a)p(a)\right]x\\
&= \sum_x p(x)x\\
&= \mathbb{E}[x]
\end{aligned}
$$

我们可以扩展得到如下结论

$$
\mathbb{E}[X\mid A=a] = \sum_b \mathbb{E}[X\mid A=a, B=b]p(b\mid a)
$$
