Gestures are used to build deeper connections between you and the audience.
# Great tips for your body
1. Posture breeds success
2. Gestures strengthen our message
3. The audience's body matters, too

# Posture breeds success
- Stand in the center of the stage, not somewhere that may distract audience
- Face the audience
- Do not put hands in pockets
- Do not put hands on hips
- Do not fig-leaf

# Gestures 
- The give, is used to give the audience the facts, or the options, have your palms up.
- The show. Diverse, but the message and the gesture should match.
- The chop, used to deliver strong opinion.


> Avoid the finger wag: Never use your fingers at your audience.
{: .prompt-warning }

# Engage with the audience better
- Using the pre-mentioned body languages
- Notice. Look for eye-contact.
- Cold call.
